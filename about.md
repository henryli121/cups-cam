## About CUPS

Cambridge University Photography Society (CUPS) was established in 2025 to foster a creative and inclusive community for all University of Cambridge students interested in photography.

### Our Mission

To provide a welcoming environment for students to explore, learn, and share their passion for photography, whether using professional cameras or mobile phones.

### What We Offer

- Photography workshops and tutorials
- Photowalks around Cambridge and beyond
- Exhibitions and showcases
- Social events and community building
